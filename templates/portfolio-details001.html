Case Study: The Rise of WeMakeCoders

Let's Work Together

About This Project

In 2018, a small group of passionate software developers started offering training to their friends and family. By 2019, with the support of SoftwareStudios, they established their own company, WeMakeCoders. We even provided them with their domain for free—just a little added touch of support! Fast forward six years, and WeMakeCoders has grown from a team of 2 to 20, with student enrollment skyrocketing from 1 to 1,000. Their sales have surged from ₹30,000 to ₹30 lakhs, showcasing the transformative power of digital marketing and technology.

Client : WeMakeCoders

Date : June 1, 2024

Category : Educational Growth

How WeMakeCoders Achieved Remarkable Growth

What started as a small training initiative for friends and family in 2018 evolved into a thriving educational enterprise with the support of SoftwareStudios. By 2019, WeMakeCoders was born, benefiting from our assistance in setting up their online presence and digital marketing strategies. Over the past six years, their growth has been nothing short of extraordinary.

The journey from 2 team members to 20, and from 1 to 1,000 students, reflects the immense impact of effective digital solutions. Their sales growth from ₹30,000 to ₹30 lakhs underscores the success of their strategic expansion and the robust partnership with SoftwareStudios.

Their success story highlights the power of technology and digital marketing in transforming a small-scale initiative into a major educational player, driving significant impact and reach.

The Final Impact

WeMakeCoders' journey from a small local initiative to a prominent educational institution is a testament to the effectiveness of strategic digital marketing and technology. With SoftwareStudios’s support, they have achieved remarkable growth, successfully training thousands of students and establishing strong industry partnerships. Their story is a powerful example of how digital transformation can drive substantial business success.
